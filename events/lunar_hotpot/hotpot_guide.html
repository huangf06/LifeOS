Hotpot Cooking Guide

Wait for the broth to boil, then add ingredients and cook to your liking!

🥩 Meat

Beef slices (thin)10-15 sec
Lamb slices (thin)10-15 sec
Shrimp2-3 min
Fish balls3-5 min
Surimi / fishcake2-3 min

🥬 Vegetables

Napa cabbage2-3 min
Spinach30-60 sec
Corn5-8 min
Potato slices3-5 min
Tofu3-5 min

🍄 Mushrooms

Enoki mushrooms1-2 min
Shiitake mushrooms3-5 min
Wood ear mushrooms3-5 min

🍜 Noodles & Starches

Glass noodles (vermicelli)3-5 min
Udon noodles3-5 min
Egg noodles3-5 min
Dumplings (frozen)5-8 min

💡 Tips

  • Thin meat slices — just swish a few times in the broth and they're done. Overcooking makes them tough!
  • Leafy greens — add last, they cook fast and shrink a lot
  • Mushrooms & root veggies — put in early, they need more time
  • Noodles — save for the end! The broth is richest after cooking all the other ingredients
  • Dipping sauce — mix your own: sesame paste + soy sauce + chili oil + garlic + vinegar + cilantro
  • Raw vs. cooked — use the shared serving chopsticks to put raw food in the pot, and your own chopsticks to eat
  • Don't overcrowd the pot — cook in small batches for best results
