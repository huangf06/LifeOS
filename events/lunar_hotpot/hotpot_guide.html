福Hotpot Cooking Guide

3 broths: 🔴 Spicy Oil (hot!) · 🟠 Tomato (mild) · 🟡 Mushroom (mild)
Wait for the broth to boil, then add ingredients!

Beef rib eye slices10-15 sec
Lamb slices10-15 sec
Shrimp paste (spoon into broth)2-3 min
Tilapia fillets2-3 min
Surimi sticks2-3 min

Beef meatballs4-5 min
Mushroom pork balls4-5 min
Crispy pork (add late!)1-2 min

Kelp3-5 min

Sweet potato noodles3-5 min

🥟 Dumplings & xiao long bao served separately!

💡 Tips

  • Prep ahead — wood ear mushrooms & kelp need to be soaked in water for 30 min before cooking
  • Thin meat slices — just swish a few times in the broth and they're done. Overcooking makes them tough!
  • Shrimp paste — scoop with a spoon and gently drop into the broth, wait until it floats
  • Crispy pork & dough sticks — add near the end, they soak up broth beautifully but get soggy fast
  • Noodles — save for the end! The broth is richest after cooking all the other ingredients
  • Dipping sauce — we have pre-made bags (original & spicy). Add vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, or wasabi to taste
  • Hygiene — use the shared serving chopsticks to put raw food in the pot, your own chopsticks to eat
  • Don't overcrowd the pot — cook in small batches for best results
