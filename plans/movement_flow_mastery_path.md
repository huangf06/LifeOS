# Movement Flow Mastery Path: Body Restoration & Awakening

**Goal:** Develop spontaneous, fluid flow sequences through progressive mastery of Movement Flow system
**Focus:** Body restoration, movement awakening, and internalized pattern recognition
**Timeline:** 12-16 weeks (adapt based on your progress)

---

## Core Philosophy: The P.I.C. Process

1. **PREPARATION** - Build strength and flexibility foundations
2. **INTEGRATION** - Combine movements with smooth transitions
3. **CREATION** - Move freely with spontaneous expression

---

## Phase 1: FOUNDATION - Body Awakening (Weeks 1-4)

**Focus:** Safety Range movements, basic body awareness, The Four Cups balance

### Week 1-2: Ground Connection & Mobility
**Daily Practice (20-30 min):**
- Warm-up sequence (from manual p.17)
  - Arm snaps: 20 reps
  - Squat snaps: 20 reps
  - Downdog leg lifts: 20 reps each leg
  - Sissy squat: 10 reps
  - Puppy pose: 30 sec hold
  - Wheel push ups: 10 sec hold
  - Wrist & shoulder prep: 10 reps

**Core Movements to Master:**
1. **Slide Ups** (easiest entry point)
   - Straddle Up (p.48-49)
   - Focus: Palm rotation, straight arm guidance
   - Reps: 5 each side, slow and controlled

2. **Hip Rolls**
   - Side Hip Roll (p.114-115)
   - Focus: Absorption, hip control
   - Reps: 3 each side

3. **Basic Rolls**
   - Forward Roll (first half only, p.74-75)
   - Focus: Spinal flexion, safe rolling
   - Reps: 5 times

**Restoration Emphasis:**
- Move slowly, breathe deeply
- Focus on "Absorption" principle - silent, controlled movements
- Daily mobility: Straddle pancake, two knee twist, side stretch (30 sec each)

---

### Week 3-4: Expanding Range
**Add to daily practice:**

4. **Sliding Splits**
   - Forward Fold Sliding Split (p.56-57)
   - Focus: Hip rotation, eye guidance
   - Reps: 3 each side

5. **Matrix Foundation**
   - Canoe (p.123-124)
   - Focus: Hip flexor opening, weight shifting
   - Reps: 5 times

6. **Basic Transitions**
   - Practice linking: Slide Up ‚Üí Hip Roll ‚Üí Slide Up
   - 3 rounds of this mini-flow

**Checkpoint:** Can you perform each movement with control and silence?

## Key Principles Throughout Your Journey

### The Four Cups - Keep Balanced:
- **Strength Cup** (50%): Build through holds and controlled movements
- **Flexibility Cup** (60%): Expand through daily stretching
- **Power Cup** (78%): Develop through dynamic transitions
- **Control Cup** (83%): Master through slow, precise practice

### The Three Ranges:
- **Weeks 1-4:** Safety Range (natural protection)
- **Weeks 5-8:** Peripheral Range (cautiously exploring limits)
- **Weeks 9-16:** Dynamic Range (smooth, controlled beyond limits)

### Small Details That Matter:
1. **Silence** - Your movements should be quiet (absorption principle)
2. **Pointed toes** - Creates lightness and control
3. **Eye guidance** - Where you look is where you go
4. **Palm rotation** - Softens angles, enables fluidity
5. **Absorption** - Roll through joints, distribute impact
